On UBC campus, over one million single use coffee cups become waste every year.

UBC has a goal to decrease the amount of waste being disposed to landfill every year, toward zero waste.

UBC is committed to recycling or composting 80% of its waste by 2020. Help us reach that goal by sorting it out!

Most products and packaging sold at UBC are recyclable or compostable, rather than garbage.

Sorting food scraps into the green bin keeps those materials out of the landfill and helps protect the climate.

About this Game

The main purpose of this game is to help faculty, alumni, and current students at UBC learn about our unique sorting system. You may be suprised at how some items need to be sorted. Alongside the goal of educating, this game also will collect data about how users sort common items, helping UBC better understand behaviours and attempt to rectify them through various initiatives.
